THE ROOTS

My grandmother
didn't know apps.

My roots come from Iran, Lebanon, Turkey. Cultures that have known for thousands of years: the body speaks. Nature answers. Herbs, rituals, silence — this wasn't alternative medicine. It was everyday life. Knowledge passed from mother to daughter — without books, without explanation, because the body needs no explanation.

I grew up with this. With this intuition. This deep conviction that the body is always transmitting — long before we listen. On my own initiative, I educated myself: NLP, stress management, wellbeing. Not because anyone demanded it — but because I wanted to understand. Myself. My body. The people around me.

Thousands of years of wisdom. The precision of today. Hamburg, 2026.

THE PROBLEM

A separate tool
for everything.

Sleep problems? There's an app. Stress? A different device. HRV? Something else entirely. I know this not just from my work — I know it from my own life. For years. Every day.

No system that thinks together what belongs together. No system that truly understands — not just measures, not just displays, but acts.

This isn't a technical problem. It's a human one. I saw it every day — in myself, in my mentees, in teenagers who couldn't sleep anymore at 17. In people who only break down when it's too late.

THE MOMENT

Then came the moment that changed everything.

My mother had a brain hemorrhage.
I called the ambulance.
I waited.
I was still waiting.

Nearly thirty minutes.
I could only wait.

Later I learned: the warning signs were there.
Weeks before. In her sleep. In her blood pressure.
In small patterns that no one had thought together.

The system could have seen them.
The system didn't exist.

THE ANSWER

So I'm building it.

NANIL Pulse is not a product. It's an answer — to thirty minutes of waiting. To 35 years of knowing the right thing, but not having the right tool. To generations of wisdom that no app contains.

A system that truly understands your body. That recognizes patterns before you see them. That doesn't alarm — but regulates. Gently. Precisely. Automatically.

"Never scare, always soothe."
